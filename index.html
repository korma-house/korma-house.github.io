About us


Korma House is Leamington’s newest restaurant, specialising in authentic North & South Indian cuisine.  Inspired by the true taste of Indian cuisine, our menu is unique, with each dish specially chosen by our chefs who have more than 35 years of experience.

Located in the heart of Royal Leamington Spa, Korma House consists of a large dining area with an elegant interior and one stylish Lounge Bar.

The atmosphere here is at once welcoming and relaxing, but subtle enough not to distract from the food itself. For the well-being of our customers and the environment, we try our best to use local, wholesome, free-range and organic ingredients where ever possible.
